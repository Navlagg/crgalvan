History


Main Page History Products Community

In the early 2000, founder and current CEO of Razer, Min-Liang Tan, and Robert Krakoff, the GM of karna which closed down in the year 2000, using technology from karna including mechanical high speed tracking worked together with engineers to create the Razer Boomslang. A team of marketers proceeded on selling this high end gaming mouse and swept the gaming industry with its innovative design.



(Displayed above is the Razer Boomslang)

2005 - Min and Robert acquire the rights from kärna to the Razer brand and establish Razer Inc in San Diego, California.
2006 - Razer establishes the regional Asia-Pacific HQ in Singapore.
2007 - Razer expands to Europe and establishes regional European HQ in Hamburg, Germany.
2008 - Razer opens R&D center in San Francisco, California.

In 2009, Engineered with world’s first gaming-grade wireless technology, the Razer Mamba also makes headlines as the fastest mouse of its time.


Razer Mamba

Recent accomplishments in 2015 includes Razer celebrating 10th year founding anniversary. as well as "Razer wins the Best of CES – People’s Choice Award for the fifth year running and snags ‘Best Gaming’ with the Android TV micro-console, Razer Forge TV."
